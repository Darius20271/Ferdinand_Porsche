Volkswagen Beetle

Designed in the 1930s under Adolf Hitler's direction to create a "people's car," the Volkswagen Beetle became one of the most iconic vehicles in history. Key Features:

  • Rear-engine, air-cooled design for reliability.
  • Affordable and durable, reshaping the automotive industry.
With over 21 million units produced, it remains a symbol of Ferdinand Porsche's ingenuity.
