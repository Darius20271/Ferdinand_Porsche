Old Age of Ferdinand Porsche

During World War II, Ferdinand Porsche developed military vehicles and played a key role in the design of the famous Volkswagen Beetle. After the war, he was briefly imprisoned but soon joined his son, Ferry Porsche, to create the legendary Porsche 356 in 1948. Ferdinand Porsche passed away on January 30, 1951, leaving behind a legacy of automotive brilliance that continues to influence the industry to this day.
